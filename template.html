Study & Research

After intensive market study and research, along with surveys done over the last few years, we noticed that there has been a noticeable increase in the demand for healthier and smokeless tobacco. People globally have become aware about the hazardous effects of smoking and are exploring safer ways of satiating their urge for nicotine. In a recent study done by BMC Medicine, the market for smokeless tobacco is of 300 million user. And it is expanding rapidly.

The state-of–the-art Technology

According to numerous researches, Filter Khaini is potentially one of the safest methods of smokeless tobacco as it acts as a barrier between tobacco and the consumer. This technology does not let consumers digest the substance and rather lets consumers get the desired flavor along with the nicotine kick.
